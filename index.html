Welcome to Our Sacred Temple

✦

The Muriyathottam Devi Amma Temple is an ancient and revered place of worship nestled in Vizhinjam, Kerala. For many generations, this temple has served as a spiritual beacon of strength, protection, and blessings for thousands of devoted families.

The presiding deity, Śrī Bhadrakāḷī Devi, is worshipped here with deep devotion through traditional Tantric rituals that have been carefully preserved over the centuries.

Devotees visit the temple seeking the Goddess's blessings for good health, protection from adversity, prosperity, and the well-being of their families. The temple remains a living centre of faith, culture, and community.

About the Temple

✦

Śrī Bhadrakāḷī — the fierce and compassionate form of the Great Mother Goddess — is the principal deity of this kshetram. She is the embodiment of courage, protection, and divine justice. Devotees offer prayers, flowers, lamps, and special sevas to seek her blessings and protection.

This temple follows the ancient Tantric traditions of Kerala, with rituals and customs performed exactly as handed down through generations of priests. The sacred atmosphere, the fragrance of incense, and the glow of oil lamps create an experience of profound peace and devotion.

The temple holds great significance for the local community and is the centre of important festivals and collective prayers throughout the year.

Deity — Śrī Bhadrakāḷī
Tradition — Kerala Tantric
Location — Vizhinjam, Kerala
